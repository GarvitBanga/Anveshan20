About Anveshan

The Metallurgy Society and the Department of Metallurgical Engineering, IIT (BHU) presents the 7th edition of the Annual Technical Meet – Anveshan ’20: Forged by Fire.

Anveshan ’20 aims to promote and foster a collaborative bridge between academics, research organizations and industries to address current and future societal challenges of the 21st century. It provides students across the nation opportunities to shape themselves into the next generation of leaders who understand the complexities of technology and society and to use their intellectual resources and innovative thinking to meet the challenges of the present and future generations.

This technical meet will provide a platform for students to contemplate guest speaker talks; showcase and talk about their projects and research works; reach out to the industries at large by acquainting them with real life problems faced therein; and grab internships under distinguished researchers, meanwhile honing their presentation & communication skills through paper and poster presentation events.

Anveshan showcases the broad spectrum of the field of metallurgical engineering and materials science that is one of the core strengths of our Institute.
