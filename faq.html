FAQs


1.Why registration is necessary?

Registering on the website will generate a unique ID number for you. This ID number is essential for participation or entry to all the activities going on during Anveshan! If you want to register for a competition or accommodation or attend guest lectures against your college ID, you’ll need to have an ID number. Your ID number is the key to the best things the festival has to offer.

7.Why should I give my information in registration?

All the details asked are relevant and are used to keep you updated about events at Anveshan. All the information is kept safe within the Anveshan Database.


8.What to do after completing the registration?

You will receive an mail regarding the confirmation of registration for Anveshan which will contain ID number. After this, all you need to do is attend Anveshan :D .


9.Do I need to register separately for all the events?

No, your registration(Register for events here!) include all the 8 events and guest lectures.


10.Where will the accommodation be provided?

Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well-secured separate residential complexes on the campus of IIT BHU.


11.Does the registration fee include the food facility as well?

No. The registration don’t include food. However, there will be food courts operational during Anveshan to cater to the food requirements.


12.What all does the Registration fee include?

The accommodation cost includes staying within the campus during the festival, direct entry to guest lectures without passes, Hospitality kit, entry to all the Anveshan events.


13.Is there any fee to attend the workshops during Anveshan?

Yes, there are separate but genuine charges. To know the workshop topics stay tuned to FB and Instagram.


14.What is the procedure to be followed after we reach IIT BHU?

You need to come to the Registration Desk, at the Department of Metallurgy along with your college identity card. You will be allotted your place to stay on campus and given a registration booklet. In the case of a big contingent, a contingent leader needs to present the ID cards of all the people in his contingent at the accommodation desk along with a list of all the people. Accommodation would be strictly on a shared basis.


15.I am not traveling with my college contingent and will be arriving separately. what should I do?

You need to proceed with the registration in a similar way. You need to come to the Registration Desk, at the Department of Metallurgy along with your college identity card. You will be allotted your place to stay on campus and given a registration booklet.


16.What are the nearest railway stations and airport to reach IIT BHU?

Varanasi Cantt Railway Station,Manduadih Railway Station.


17.How will I get to know about timings and venues for the events during Anveshan?

You will be given a registration booklet that will have all the timings of all the events and their venues.
