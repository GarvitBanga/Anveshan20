Event Description

Internado focuses on providing students a competition to display their knowledge to win the career making internships under distinguished scientists, pioneer industrialists and reverent professors from best organizations across the country.

The participants will be given problems in varied project categories. The winners will be offered internships in the industry or the research institute for which the concerned participant has provided the solution.

Event Details

  • The selected students for each problem statement under various categories will get internships offer under the respective internship provider.
  • The internship providers may even conduct interviews with the best candidates as final selection criteria.
