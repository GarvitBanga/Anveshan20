Event Description

To highlight the importance of microscopy and its significance in developing new materials, Anveshan'20 presents - Microskopia, a metallography-microscopy event aims to fire up budding materials scientists by providing them with a venture to marvel their scientific observation skills and nudging them to fathom their knowledge of microscopy.

Please note that no Problem Statement for this event will be communicated. Participants have to present their work in metallography-microscopy.


Judging Criteria

The evaluation will be based on-

  • Electronic report
  • Presentation
  • Overall impact of the work
  • Novelty/innovation in the work

Each member will be given a total of 10 minutes for presentation. The presentation should cover aspects related to the novelty/innovation and the impact of the work in that field.


Event Details

  • Participants must submit their micrographs in electronic format and mail it to anveshan@iitbhu.ac.in.
  • The word limit for the report is 2000.
  • The report must include references whenever required. Plagiarism would result in immediate disqualification from the event.
  • The top three entries would feature in the sessional Newsletter of Department of Metallurgical Engineering at IIT (BHU), Varanasi.
